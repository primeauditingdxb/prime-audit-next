## Overview of UAE Corporate Tax Framework

### Understanding UAE Corporate Tax in Dubai

The UAE introduced corporate tax at a standard rate of **9% on taxable income exceeding AED 375,000**. While the rate may appear straightforward, compliance involves:

- Corporate tax registration
- Determining taxable income
- Transfer pricing documentation
- Proper financial reporting
- Filing corporate tax returns
- Maintaining records for audit readiness

Under guidance from the Ministry of Finance, businesses must comply with specific regulations, exemptions, and reporting standards.

Here’s the reality: **the complexity lies not in the percentage, but in interpretation.**
Even small classification errors or documentation gaps can trigger fines from the FTA.
