## Understanding Corporate Tax in Dubai

Corporate tax in the UAE is regulated by the Federal Tax Authority (FTA) and applies to businesses exceeding the taxable income threshold.

According to the Ministry of Finance UAE, businesses must:
- Register for corporate tax
- Maintain audited financial records
- File annual tax returns
- Ensure proper documentation of deductible expenses

Failure to comply can result in administrative penalties and financial scrutiny. For SMEs and startups in Dubai, navigating these regulations without structured **Tax Advisory Services** can quickly become overwhelming.
