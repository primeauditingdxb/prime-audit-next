## Understanding VAT Compliance in Dubai

The UAE introduced VAT in 2018 under the supervision of the Federal Tax Authority. Businesses exceeding the mandatory registration threshold must do the following:
- Register for VAT
- Charge 5% VAT on taxable supplies
- Maintain proper financial records
- File VAT returns quarterly (or monthly)
- Pay VAT on time

Failure to comply can lead to fines, audits, and reputational risk. According to guidelines from the Federal Tax Authority, businesses must retain records for at least five years. That includes invoices, credit notes, import/export documents, and accounting books.

Here’s the reality: manual spreadsheets are rarely enough.
