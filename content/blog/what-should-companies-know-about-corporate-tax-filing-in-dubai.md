## What Is Corporate Tax in the UAE?

Corporate tax is a 9% federal tax applied to business profits above the UAE’s exempt threshold.
It applies to:
- Mainland companies
- Free zone companies (with specific rules)
- Foreign entities with UAE-sourced income
- Freelancers earning business income

Accurate filing isn’t optional, it’s legally required.

## Corporate Tax Filing Deadlines in the UAE

Most companies must file their corporate tax return within 9 months of the end of their financial year.

**Example:**
If your financial year ends on 31 December, your tax filing deadline is 30 September.
