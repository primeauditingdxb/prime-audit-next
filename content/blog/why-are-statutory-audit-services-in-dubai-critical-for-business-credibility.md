## What Is a Statutory Audit, and Who Needs It?

A statutory audit is an independent examination of a company’s financial statements to verify accuracy, compliance, and fairness.

**In Dubai, statutory audits are typically required for:**
- Mainland LLCs (as per Memorandum of Association requirements)
- Free zone companies (mandatory annual submission)
- Companies seeking investor funding
- Businesses applying for bank financing
- Firms preparing for mergers or acquisitions

For example, a startup in Dubai Silicon Oasis preparing for Series A funding will almost certainly need audited financial statements before investor due diligence begins.

**Investors want numbers they can trust, not spreadsheets created in-house.**

Most Dubai free zones require annual audits for license renewal and regulatory compliance.
