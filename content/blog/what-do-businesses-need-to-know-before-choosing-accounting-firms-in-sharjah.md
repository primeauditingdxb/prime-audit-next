## UAE Compliance & Regulatory Requirements

The UAE introduced VAT in 2018 under the Federal Tax Authority (FTA). Since then, compliance has become non-negotiable.

**Businesses operating in Sharjah and Dubai must follow:**
- VAT registration and filing rules
- Corporate tax regulations (where applicable)
- Financial record maintenance (minimum 5 years)
- Payroll documentation
- ESR (Economic Substance Regulations) where relevant

According to the Federal Tax Authority, inaccurate VAT filing can result in significant penalties.

**Professional accounting firms in Sharjah ensure:**
- Timely VAT returns
- Proper documentation
- FTA-compliant reporting
- Reduced risk of fines

Skipping this step can cost far more than outsourcing accounting.
