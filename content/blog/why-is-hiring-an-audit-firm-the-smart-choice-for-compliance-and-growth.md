## Ensure Full VAT and Corporate Tax Compliance

Since VAT was introduced in 2018 and corporate tax frameworks were implemented in 2023, financial reporting standards in the UAE have significantly tightened.

Businesses must:
- Maintain proper transaction records
- Classify taxable and exempt supplies correctly
- File VAT returns accurately and on time
- Assess corporate tax liabilities properly
- Retain documentation for regulatory review

One SME in Jumeirah approached an audit firm after discovering inconsistencies in VAT filings prepared internally. A structured review corrected classification errors before penalties escalated. Compliance isn’t optional in Dubai’s ecosystem. It’s fundamental.
