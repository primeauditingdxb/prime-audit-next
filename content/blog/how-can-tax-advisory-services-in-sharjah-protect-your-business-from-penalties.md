## Understanding Tax Compliance in Sharjah

The UAE tax landscape has evolved rapidly over the past few years. Businesses in Sharjah must now comply with:
- VAT registration and filing requirements
- Corporate tax registration and annual returns
- Economic Substance Regulations (ESR)
- Anti-Money Laundering (AML) compliance

Failure to meet deadlines or submit accurate records can trigger penalties from the Federal Tax Authority. Here’s the reality: many penalties occur not because of deliberate wrongdoing but because of misunderstanding regulations.

Common pitfalls include:
- Incorrect VAT calculations
- Late return submissions
- Misclassified zero-rated or exempt supplies
- Poor record-keeping
