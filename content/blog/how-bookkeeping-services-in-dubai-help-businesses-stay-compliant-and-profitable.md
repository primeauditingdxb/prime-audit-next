## What Makes Dubai’s Business Environment Financially Unique?

Dubai isn’t a typical market. Businesses here often manage:

- Free zone and mainland regulatory differences

The Ministry of Economy requires companies to maintain financial statements that accurately reflect their financial position. Non-compliance can result in penalties, trade license complications, or audit red flags.
